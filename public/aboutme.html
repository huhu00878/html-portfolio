About Hubert

Hubuert Wu

He grew up in a small town in Taiwan. Other than English, he can speak Mandarin and Taiwanese. In Taiwan, he majored in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. After he received his Master's Degree from National Taiwan University, he worked as a tutor teaching Math, Science, and Electronics. Because he had to handle students' emotions and academic questions by listening carefully, it trained his patience and communication skills.

In 2017, he moved to Vancouver, and decided to follow his passion for food and cooking. A year later, he applied to study culinary arts at Vancouver Community College (VCC). In 2019, he got into VCC and began a two-year culinary course. Before Covid-19 pandemic, he volunteered several times as a kitchen Helper at VCC. Moreover, he has experience working in a busy kitchen because of his part-time job. On April first, 2021, he graduated from VCC. He had an honor to join as a line cook at a 5-star hotel (Wedgewood Hotel & Spa). Later, he moved to an Italian Care Home (Villa Carital) to keep improving his cooking skill and strengthen the concept of European cuisine. The knowledge and experience from VCC, the Hotel, and the Care Home have given him more confidence in his culinary career.

He hopes to keep learning and broaden horizon. He seizes every learning opportunity, because he believes "Chance favors the prepared mind".
